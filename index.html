About the Project

ME CHAOS SUPREME is a dark satirical film exploring ideology, dystopia, and digital power —
produced anonymously by Bobby Freeman in collaboration with Chaos Studios. This is a work of fiction.
All resemblance to real persons, events, or institutions is purely intentional and absurd.

Harmony Sculpture Reveal

“CHAOS flipped its wings back to harmony.”

Forged from scrapped MAFA metal, the Harmony Sculpture emits soft green and blue light— a symbol of Gaia's breath returning to the world. Its spiral form rises from the ruins, topped by a pierced red MAFA hat, now redefined as a relic of chaos transcended. In the stillness, it hums... a soothing soundscape echoing the end of propaganda, and the rebirth of balance.
